How it begins

  • A ship falls from the sky.
  • Cassandra's best friend, Lillia, collapses with violet eyes.
  • A hospital becomes a slaughterhouse.
  • A cult rises worshipping a mysterious Blonde Woman.
  • In a moment of emotional rage, Cassandra pushes the Blonde Woman's head into a tear of reality.
  • Lillia and The Blonde Woman merge into one being.
  • Lillia becomes her.
  • Persephone is her.
  • They are Persephone.
  • It's their world. Reality just lives in it.
